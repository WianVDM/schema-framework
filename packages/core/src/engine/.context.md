## Violations (Pre-Rule)

- `types.ts` exports 20+ types/interfaces - should be split into individual files (e.g., `field-type.ts`, `field-schema.ts`, `form-schema.ts`, etc.)
- `validators.ts` exports 4 validators, 3 inferred types, 1 interface, and 4 functions - should be split into individual files
- `primitives-context.tsx` exports 3 symbols - should be split into `primitives-context.ts`, `primitives-provider.tsx`, `use-primitives.ts`
- `theme-provider.tsx` exports 2 symbols - should be split into `theme-provider.tsx` and `use-theme.ts`
